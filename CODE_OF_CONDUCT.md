# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants in this project are expected to be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements in technical terms
- Focus reviews on code, behavior, and evidence
- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds
- Keep discussions relevant to improving the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or demeaning language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove content, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.
